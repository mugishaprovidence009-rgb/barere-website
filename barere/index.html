The Challenge

Too many children lose school to poverty.

Not because they can't learn, but because their families can't afford the basics.

What We Do

A whole child model that keeps children in school.

We do not just get children back into class. We build the home and community conditions that let them stay.

Pillar 01

Education Support

Tuition, uniforms, supplies, and school feeding, everything a child needs to enroll and stay in school.

Mentorship

Our values

1

The Whole Child

We support the whole child: school, home, heart, and future, not just tuition.

2

Family at the Center

Lasting change for a child begins with a stronger, more stable family.

3

Passing It On

Every family we lift up goes on to lift up the next, in a cycle of shared responsibility.

4

Every Child, Everywhere
