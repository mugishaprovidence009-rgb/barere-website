The people behind Barere.

Barere is powered by a small but growing team — and by the guardians, teachers, and partners who make every day of action possible.

We are just getting started. Barere began with one belief — that no child should lose their education to poverty — and it is growing into a team of people who share it. Here is who is making Barere happen today.

Founder

Providence Mugisha

Providence founded Barere in June 2025 to make sure children who had been pushed out of school by poverty could return — and stay. She leads the program's vision, its work on the ground, and the partnerships that keep it growing.
