Vision Ahead

Three goals for the next chapter

From returning to school, to never leaving again: deepening our work with the children already enrolled, and strengthening their families' ability to provide stable homes.

23

Students kept in school

Full annual tuition, school feeding, and supplies covered so every child stays enrolled.

23

Family pig enterprises

One pig per family, plus shelters, feed, veterinary care, and farming training, as a lasting income source rather than a one time gift.

∞

A self sustaining cycle

Each family passes piglets forward, so every new cohort is empowered by the last.

The Long Term Vision

Every district. Every child.

Barere began in Kibuye, but the need reaches far beyond it. Our long term vision is to grow this whole child model into all 30 districts of Rwanda, so that no child anywhere in the country is forced out of school by poverty.
