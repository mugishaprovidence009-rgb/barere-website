The Long-Term Vision

Every district. Every child.

Barere began in Kibuye — but the need reaches far beyond it. Our long-term vision is to grow this whole-child model into all 30 districts of Rwanda, so that no child anywhere in the country is forced out of school by poverty.

Education

Keeping every child in the classroom.

Barere will ensure students remain in formal education by covering annual tuition, school feeding costs, and required supplies — removing the financial barriers that so often force vulnerable children out of school.

Family Livelihood

A family livestock initiative provides each participating family with a pig, shelter, feed, veterinary services, and practical farming workshops — with training in care, hygiene, breeding, and income management so the pig becomes a lasting source of income.

Mentorship

Continued workshops and one-on-one sessions focused on emotional wellness, goal-setting, school motivation, family relationships, and life skills — listening to each child individually and responding to their specific needs.
